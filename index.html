About Me

My name is Quintin, and I am currently a sophomore Computer Science student at York College of Pennsylvania. I am passionate about computers, writing, and hearing myself talk. When I'm not occupied with schoolwork, I am an amateur table tennis champion, professional shower concert performer, and a motivated learner. I also tutor fellow Computer Science students.

What I Do

I have an incomprehensibly huge passion for making websites. From the early days of my uncle giving me a spiral-bound HTML book and making basic, plain web pages with blinding white backgrounds and unorganized, unstyled text to practicing every day after school during my senior year of high school, I have always had a love for seeing my concepts come to life on web pages. Website development is one of the most fulfilling things I have ever done, and I feel that I will forever continue to adore it as a hobby.

I am interested in helping those seeking to imprint their presence online. Whether you are a college student, startup, or small business, I am more than happy to discuss the possibilities with you! If you would like more information or have any questions, please contact me at quintin@quintinherb.net.
